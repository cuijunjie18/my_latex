\raggedright(1)电路的输出方程：\(Y = Q_2\) \\
\quad \\
\raggedright(2)电路的驱动方程：
$
\begin{cases}

    D_0 = Q_2^{'}Q_1^{'}Q_0^{'} \\
    D_1 = Q_0 \\
    D_2 = Q_1 

\end{cases}
$\\ 
\quad \\
\raggedright(3)电路的状态方程：\\
由D触发器的特性方程\(Q = D\)有\\
\begin{align}
    Q_0 &= D_0 = Q_2^{'}Q_1^{'}Q_0^{'} \nonumber \\
    Q_1 &= D_1 = Q_0 \nonumber \\
    Q_2 &= D_2 = Q_1 \nonumber
\end{align}
\raggedright 故电路的状态方程为：
$
\begin{cases}
    Q_0 =Q_2^{'}Q_1^{'}Q_0^{'} \\ 
    Q_1 = Q_0 \\ 
    Q_2 = Q_1 \\
\end{cases}
$\\
\; \\
\; \\
